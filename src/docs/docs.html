About

Habit Alarm is a simple productivity extension designed to reward your productive actions with browsing time. You start off with zero minutes and, upon performing a good habit or completing a task, you can give yourself a desired amount of time to continue browsing distracting websites.

How to Use

(Un)Blacklisting Websites

To blacklist websites, simply go to a desired website, click the badge icon in the top right of your browser , go to the settings page , and click "Blacklist this website". Likewise, to unblacklist the website, click "Unblacklist this website". If you desire to clear your blacklist, click "Clear Blacklist".

Note: Websites are blacklisted on a domain-name basis. For example, if your current page's url is "https://website.com/subpage", https://website.com will be blacklisted and any other subpages will reduce browsing time. Blacklisting is only designed for http://, https://, and domain-first urls (for example, website.com)! Anything else will not work properly, but for all practical purposes every website will be able to be blacklisted.

Giving Yourself or Taking Away Time

To reward your productive actions with browsing time, click the badge icon in the top right of your browser , go to the addition/subtraction page, and click any desired minute count (+5, +15, and +30) to add to your total browsing time.

If you accidentally added time or want to punish yourself for something (not recommended!), click any desired minute count (-5, -15, and -30) you wish to deduct from your total browsing time. Additionally, "Clear time" can be clicked to reset your browsing time to zero.
